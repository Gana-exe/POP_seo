About POP

Not just Promises..

Our Mission

Align measurable results, growth, seamless protocols and customer satisfaction to bring out the positive transformation that each brand deserves.

Our Vision

One day, we see us building a network of powerful brands who reach their marketing destinations.

“Live a life you’ll remember” - Avicii

Marketing is an ocean and it takes a lifetime to swim the vast expanse, but with Point One it gets just a little better. We are Vijay Anand G and Abishek Venkatesh, two aspiring content marketers determined to partner with businesses that make the world a better place. Founded in 2018, Point One Promotions has grown with over 50 clients in the last year. As a fully functional branding and advertising agency, Point One has handled over 600+ campaigns with a growing team of digital marketers. We have an established office in the bustles of Chennai that caters to a variety of verticals across the country.
