Who We Are

As a family of content drivers and innovative designers, Point One Promotions operates out of Chennai intending to deliver a resilient online presence strategy that is equipped to meet the ever-changing dynamics of the digital world.

Success With ‘Point Marketing’

We strongly believe that finding the target audience is the key to unlock success and growth. Point One Promotions is a stickler for market research which allows us to understand our clients’ target audience better. Our ‘Point’ marketing strategy is our unique take on branding and advertising concepts that help us penetrate the market better. Using the research we try to focus all our marketing efforts on creating a ‘point’ marketing strategy to produce exact results our clients desire.

Your Goal, Achieved Our Way

Our result-driven marketers ensure all our strategies and deliveries are focused on achieving the client’s goal using the arsenal of digital weapons we possess — SEO, branding, digital marketing, and more. Depending on your need, we bring the right plan that is crafted using the basket of services we offer.

Unlock Your Digital Presence

With a cyclic vision to produce results around the year, we believe we must create a formidable digital presence that allows businesses to reach the next level. We believe in our customers and focus on realizing their dreams with our strategies.
