Vijay Anand G

Founder

Vijay’s “life is this; I like this” attitude got him to partner with his friend in his early 20s to start a fully functioning and reputed digital agency in Chennai. His Netflix binges often have him making a connection with fictional characters. Much like Harvey Specter (Suits, duh), Vijay is determined and focused on success, and right now, his eyes are only on POP. When not talking about work (which is rare), you’ll end up having discussions with him on his dog, Joey and his favourite football team - Real Madrid.

Abishek Venkatesh

Co-Founder

Much like his idol Tony Stark, our Co-Founder Abishek is all about achieving big. Abishek is a determined and driven soul of the POP team who is always focused on getting the work done. His passion for creating something for himself drove him here — to become an entrepreneur in his 20s. With the business thriving, he only spares time to catch up with his favorite football team - Manchester United!

Gowri Shankar S

Head - Marketing Operations

With expertise in Digital Marketing and Digital Growth Hacking, Gowri Shankar is our Head of Marketing Operations. Determined to succeed in business and life, Gowri is an ad campaign master, transformational change inducer, and an adherent mental health advocate. His passion for the Tamil language often turns him into a writer. When work permits him a life outside the confines of the office, he can be seen playing badminton or cricket.

Vinitha Tamilarasan

Digital Marketing Executive

One of the most dynamic personalities in the POP team, Vinitha, has extensive SEO knowledge. As the Digital Marketing Executive, she is a social media guru who decides the digital trends of our clients. Vinitha often claims that she’s bitten by the ‘Travel bug,’ making her wander off to explore new places off-duty. A self-proclaimed chef, she hones her culinary skills with family and friends, and sometimes we enjoy a little something from her cooking at work too!

Simerna Sushil

Brand Strategy & Operations Manager

A zeal in marketing with an intense eye for detail, Simerna is the Brand Strategy & Operations Manager at POP and on a constant lookout for acing the Branding game. She loves to come up with creative campaign ideas and etch successful brand stories on her wall of fame! Often found in her world of analysing thriller dramas, Simerna is also a dancer, singer, fashion blogger, pet parent, and an animal lover. She believes that a kind heart is what will take you places!

Sundar

Senior Graphic Designer

Always up to learning new things, Sundar our Senior Graphic Designer, is undoubtedly on his way to becoming one of the coolest designers at Point One Promotions. An ambitious person, he hopes to successfully re-read all the Marvel comic books. (And we are not exaggerating here). He is also a complete foodie and gadget lover at heart.

Denny

Senior Graphic Designer

Meet Denny, the Design Mafia at Point One Promotions. A hard-core gamer, he is born to win at and is remembered often in the office (we have a lot of gaming sessions in the office, trust us). Being a Senior Graphic Designer, he has honed his graphic design skills to wow the clients and is also the King of Techie at POP. He aims to be recognized amongst the top creative designers in the world.

Sanaa Parpia

Digital Marketing Manager

From writing catchy blog posts to making her Instagram photos stand out, Sanaa is quite the social media addict. As a Digital Marketing Manager, she is highly collaborative and fast in executing new campaigns and ideas. With the dream of making a mark in the Digital world, she naturally gravitated towards Marketing. After the roughest days, you will see her lighting candles around the room and breeze across the office offering smiles and hugs to anyone in her sight. In her “Me (Free) Time”, that’s what she likes to call, you can see her applying lip balm and browsing around the local places to eat.
