Stepping stones of success for a start-up

As a start-up, brand awareness and attracting the right followers was the challenge. Over one year, the goal of improving visibility was succeeded. Point One approached this requirement with a long-term objective to improve customer retention with a focus on user interactions. We believe that the key to long term user retention to a social media page is by engaging with the user beyond the metrics of likes on a post. Using various digital marketing concepts including experiential digital strategy, social media campaign, content development, content strategy, the brand’s followers count on Instagram saw a steady rise, with the current number standing over 1000 loyal followers from the Ahmedabad region is the location of the start-up.

Increasing influence on Instagram

With a clearly defined target audience, the goal was to improve brand awareness and convert profile visitors into customers. Point One reported that the key to a successful brand building exercise is trust after the initial social media audit. For the client to create a substantial social media presence, we adhered to the brand advocacy method. Using a content marketing strategy, the strategy involved a series of campaigns with testimonials for brand credibility, moment marketing for quicker visibility, and offers to improve brand inquiry. Over time, the campaign succeeded in increasing inquiries to 60 queries/month with a successful conversion rate of 30-40%. The brand’s Instagram followers grew from 400 to 1274 as a result of the campaigns conducted.

Working with the World Cup crowd

As the campaign was centered around the World Cup, Point One understood the driving forces would be the emotions and psychology of the customers. To achieve a higher success rate, we leveraged the presence of our target audience on social media platforms, including Facebook and Instagram. Using digital platforms to reach the target audience, including college students and cricket lovers, increased the chances of t-shirt sales through polls that gave insight into the preference of the TG. With only ad campaigns, improved sales around the 2019 world cup as the production was based on first-hand market research.

Capitalising Facebook pixel for audience reach

Point One conducted a successful market study and campaign roll-out based on Facebook Pixel metrics. The market study allowed the team to gather sufficient and relevant data points that could be used to build focused, result-nurturing campaigns. A successful market study and campaign roll-out based on Facebook Pixel. The data collected from Pixel offered clear insight into user persona and website user activity. Time-based ad campaigns based on this research succeeded with

  • Improvement in customer retention
  • Increase in sales to Rs. 1.6 lakhs/month
  • Delivered a surge in website traffic

Digital partnership with Google Ads as part of the launch campaign

As a way of establishing a steady relationship between buyers and sellers, Point One set up several Google ads campaigns that would help increase app downloads and brand promotion. The approach is rooted in creating a holistic campaign that would help navigate the three stages of the customer buying cycle - awareness, consideration, and conversion. Each of the campaign ideologies addressed each cycle, and the content created helped move the audience from one stage to another. The campaigns included a host of different approaches including

  • Search ads
  • Display network ads
  • YouTube ads

At the end of the campaign, the success was evaluated with 13000+ views, 11.52% clicks, and 35.19% CTR. It has to be particularly noted that the campaign garnered 6 lakhs plus impressions within the allocated budget.

Powerhouse political campaign for a better India

As the candidate prepared for elections, his digital team focused on two major areas - understanding people's behavior and bringing awareness to the candidate's political agenda. Using social media platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, Point One created an awareness campaign that helped to gain momentum among his constituents. Leveraging Gantt Charts, the digital team tracked and updated the all-day campaigns with real-time data. Over a span of 36 days, various content, including snippets of campaign and opinion polls, were used to position the candidate on the digital platform, thus creating 60 lakhs impressions before the election.
